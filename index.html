MMU — Multi-Media Uploader

MMU is a personal desktop tool that publishes short-form videos to the owner's own social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook) through each platform's official API. It runs locally on the owner's computer; it is not a hosted service, is not distributed, and is not offered to third parties.

MMU uses YouTube API Services.
By using MMU you agree to the YouTube Terms of Service. Google's privacy practices are described in the Google Privacy Policy. You can revoke MMU's access to your YouTube data at any time at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.
